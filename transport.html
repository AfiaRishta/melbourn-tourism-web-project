Melbourne Transportation Services

Melbourne offers a comprehensive transportation network, including trams, buses, and trains. Traditional taxis are readily available, and Uber operates for convenient ride-hailing. The SkyBus provides an express service between Melbourne Airport and the city center, while bike-sharing programs like Blue Bikes offer an alternative for short trips. Uber Taxis & SkyBus

You can use the Uber app to request a taxi in Melbourne. Simply call 13-UBER (13 82 37) or use the app to book a trip on demand or prebook up to 90 days in advance. Uber provides upfront prices and 24/7 availability, making it convenient for travelers. Keep in mind that taxis booked through Uber cannot pick up from airports. Traditional taxis are also available throughout Melbourne. You can hail them on the street or find taxi ranks at various locations.
